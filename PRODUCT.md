# Vision

DailyCurio exists to help people build a lifelong habit of curiosity by making learning one small, enjoyable part of every day.

Rather than overwhelming users with endless information, DailyCurio delivers meaningful discoveries that encourage reflection, spark conversations, and inspire continuous learning.

Our goal is not to help users consume more content.

Our goal is to help them become more curious people.

---

# Mission

To make learning something new every day simple, enjoyable, and sustainable.

---

# Why DailyCurio Exists

Modern technology gives people access to more information than at any other time in history.

Ironically, this abundance often leads to shallow consumption rather than meaningful learning.

People scroll continuously yet remember very little.

DailyCurio exists to reverse that pattern.

Instead of encouraging endless consumption, DailyCurio encourages intentional discovery.

One curiosity.

One moment of learning.

One day at a time.

---

# Problem Statement

Many people genuinely enjoy learning interesting things, but most educational experiences feel overwhelming, time-consuming, or disconnected from daily life.

Existing content platforms prioritize endless engagement over meaningful understanding.

DailyCurio solves this by making curiosity a simple daily habit.

---

# Value Proposition

# MVP Scope (Version 0.1.0)

The first public version should include only the essential functionality required to deliver the core value.

### Included

* Daily curiosity display.
* Clean reading interface.
* Save favourite curiosities.
* History of viewed curiosities.
* Light and dark themes.
* Responsive Flutter interface.

### Not Included

* User accounts.
* Social sharing.
* Comments.
* AI-generated content.
* Notifications.
* Gamification.
* Premium subscriptions.

These ideas may become future milestones but are intentionally excluded from Version 0.1.0.
